Biography

Md Shahriar Shakir Sumit is working as a full-time PhD research scholar at The University of New South Wales (UNSW), Canberra, Australia. He received MSc. degree (by research) in Computer and Information Sciences from Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS (UTP), Malaysia. He did his MSc. in the object detection area.

He received BSc.degree in computer science and software engineering from the American International University Bangladesh (AIUB), in 2016. He worked as a Software Engineer for two years. His research interest includes computer vision, image and video processing, object detection, tracking, missing values imputation, deep learning.
