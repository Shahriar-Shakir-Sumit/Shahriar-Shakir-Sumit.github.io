ReSTiNet: A Lightweight CNN for Designing an Efficient Human Detection System

Image understanding includes not only detecting objects in a particular scene but also categorizing the detected objects in their respective classes. Several techniques were proposed in the light of efficient extraction of features as well as classification to detect arbitrary objects in images. Deep learning techniques have effectively combined the task both extracting the features and classification in an end-to-end way. The Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) have become quite popular to tackle various problems, and object detection is one among them.This study focuses on a specific sub-domain of detection which is the human detection..
